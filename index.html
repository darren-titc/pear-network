How It Works for Hiring Companies

Our streamlined process ensures you receive the best candidates for your roles while leveraging our powerful referral network. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Purchase and Post a Job

Get started by purchasing a job slot and posting your requirements. Tailored to your industry, our platform sets you up for success.

Step 2: Network Activation

We activate our network of influencers and professionals in your industry. Shortlisted candidates are presented for review.

Step 3: Candidate Selection

Review up to 10 pre-screened profiles. Select candidates for interviews and pay a fee, part of which rewards the referrers.

Step 4: Hire and Update

Conduct interviews, make the hire, and update the system. Our platform tracks the process and ensures a seamless experience.
